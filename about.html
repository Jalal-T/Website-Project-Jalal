Facts about hockey players

Connor McDavid

Connor McDavid was drafted by the Edmonton Oilers in 2015 with the first overall pick. Since then McDavid has gotten 835 points in 559 games played.

Sidney Crosby

Sidney Crosby was selected first overall in the 2005 NHL draft by the Pittsburgh Penguins. Crosby has gotten 1494 points in 1180 games played.

Ryan Nugent-Hopkins

Ryan Nugent-Hopkins was also drafted first overall by the Edmonton Oilers in 2011. Nuge has 616 points in 791 career games played.
